Last updated: {{ current_year }}-05-15. Working draft — Tiaki uses cookies only to keep you logged in and to remember a couple of UI preferences. We do not use advertising, analytics, or cross-site tracking cookies.

In short

  • One session cookie keeps you logged in.
  • A small number of UI-preference cookies remember things like sidebar collapsed/expanded state.
  • No third-party cookies. No advertising cookies. No cross-site tracking.

Cookies we set

Essential — strictly necessary

  • session — Flask's signed session cookie. Holds your user_id, group_id, and role so the server can authorise page requests. Expires when you close your browser unless you tick "remember me" at login.

Functional — improve usability

  • sidebar_collapsed — remembers whether the sidebar was collapsed last time you used the platform. Local to your browser; never sent to third parties.

Cookies we do not set

  • Marketing or advertising cookies.
  • Cross-site tracking pixels.
  • Third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.).
  • Fingerprinting scripts.

Third-party resources

Tiaki loads a small set of resources from third-party CDNs (Bootstrap, Google Fonts, Chart.js). These providers may log a request from your browser when those files load, but the platform does not set or read cookies via those providers. You can self-host those assets if your deployment policy requires it — that change lives in the platform configuration, not the cookie policy.

Your choices

  • You can clear all Tiaki cookies through your browser's privacy settings. The next time you visit the platform you'll be asked to log in again.
  • You can block cookies for this site entirely; the platform will not work without the session cookie, so this effectively logs you out and prevents login.
  • Functional cookies (sidebar state) can be cleared without affecting login.

Changes to this policy

If we add a cookie — for example, to remember a theme picker preference or a layout choice — we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date. We will not add advertising or cross-site tracking cookies without changing the platform's privacy posture in a visible way first.

Contact

For questions about cookies on Tiaki, contact a platform administrator. A dedicated privacy contact channel will land alongside the formal launch.
